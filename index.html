Leftcraft is in downtown Edmonds.

A comfortable and creative atmosphere with well-curated offerings, dedicated to keeping things local, fresh and sustainable. Whether you’re craving homestyle comfort food or healthier options, Leftcraft celebrates the abundant ingredients throughout the region. Full bar with signature cocktails and 22 regionally-focused rotating craft selections.

West coast-inspired food and drink in a modern, neighborhood setting.

We aim to serve both lunch and dinner daily, but in the immediate future we will start with abbreviated hours as we transition through these difficult times. Open Wednesday through Sunday 12pm-9pm. No reservations currently, please call to check availability.

22 taps, rotating daily. P: 16oz pint, t: 8oz tulip, g: 12oz glass.

We would like to give as many people as possible the opportunity to visit Leftcraft.

The following policies will be in place to ensure the safety and well being of our employees and guests.

  1. Guests will be limited to 90 minutes when dining with us.
  2. Tables will be available on a first-come first-serve basis, and we can add you to our waitlist if tables are full. Feel free to give us a call to inquire about availability or to get on the list.
  3. Face coverings must be worn by guests when communicating with staff and not dining at their table.
  4. We cannot accommodate more than 5 people per table. Infants and children count as people.
  5. Indoor seating will only be available for parties from the same household.

In addition, germicidal UV lights rays will be utilized with the HVAC system. The process has been known to reduce airborne bacteria, mold, odor and virus by 99%.

Please be respectful and mindful of other patrons and staff while we navigate this process together.

We appreciate your patience and look forward to welcoming you!
